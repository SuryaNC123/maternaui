Essential Pregnancy Care Principles

General guidance that applies throughout your pregnancy journey

Emotional Well-being

Take care of your mental health

  • Practice relaxation techniques
  • Stay connected with loved ones
  • Don't hesitate to seek support
  • Be kind to yourself

Rest & Recovery

Your body needs extra rest

  • Aim for 8-9 hours of sleep nightly
  • Take short naps when needed
  • Listen to your body's signals
  • Create a relaxing bedtime routine

Nutrition

Fuel your body properly

  • Eat a balanced, varied diet
  • Stay hydrated with water
  • Take prenatal vitamins daily
  • Choose whole, nutritious foods

Physical Activity

Stay active safely

  • Exercise moderately most days
  • Try walking, swimming, or yoga
  • Avoid high-impact activities
  • Stop if you feel pain or dizzy

Medical Care

Stay in touch with your provider

  • Attend all prenatal appointments
  • Keep track of your health
  • Ask questions without hesitation
  • Follow medical recommendations

Safety First

Avoid potential risks

  • No alcohol or smoking
  • Avoid risky activities
  • Check medications with doctor
  • Practice good hygiene
